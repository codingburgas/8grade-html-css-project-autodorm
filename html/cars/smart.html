Brabus 125R

The Brabus 125R is a high-performance, limited-edition version of the Smart Fortwo Cabrio, with only 125 units produced. It features a tuned 898cc turbocharged 3-cylinder engine delivering 125 horsepower and 200 Nm of torque, enabling a 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph) acceleration in 9.2 seconds and a top speed electronically limited to 175 km/h (109 mph).

Specifications

  • Engine: 898cc turbocharged 3-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 125 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 9.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: 109 mph
  • Transmission: 6-speed dual-clutch

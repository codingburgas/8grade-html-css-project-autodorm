Snart

The Snart Electric Drive is a compact, two-seater city car designed for urban mobility. It features a fully electric powertrain with a small battery pack, offering a modest range suitable for city commuting. Its nimble size allows for easy parking and manoeuvrability in tight spaces, while providing a quiet, eco-friendly driving experience. The interior is minimalist but functional, focusing on efficiency and practicality.

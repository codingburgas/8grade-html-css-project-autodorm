Tesla Model Y

Experience the perfect blend of performance, comfort, and innovation with the Tesla Model Y. This all-electric SUV offers a spacious interior, cutting-edge technology, and impressive acceleration, making it an ideal choice for both city drives and long-distance journeys. With a range of up to 530 km on a single charge and access to Tesla's Supercharger network, your trips are both efficient and eco-friendly.

Specifications

  • Engine: Dual permanent magnet synchronous motors
  • Horsepower: 450 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 3.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph
  • Transmission: Single-speed automatic

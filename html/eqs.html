Audi RS7

The Audi RS7 is a high-performance luxury sedan that combines stunning design with exhilarating power. With its sleek lines and aggressive stance, it commands attention on the road. Under the hood, it boasts a powerful V8 engine that delivers impressive acceleration and handling, making every drive an unforgettable experience.

Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Horsepower: 630 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 3.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 190 mph
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic

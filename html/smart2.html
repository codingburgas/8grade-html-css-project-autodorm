Smart

The Smart Electric Drive is a compact, two-seater city car designed for urban mobility. It features a fully electric powertrain with a small battery pack, offering a modest range suitable for city commuting. Its nimble size allows for easy parking and manoeuvrability in tight spaces, while providing a quiet, eco-friendly driving experience. The interior is minimalist but functional, focusing on efficiency and practicality.

Specifications

  • Engine: 55 kW electric motor
  • Battery: 17.6 kWh lithium-ion battery
  • Horsepower: 74 hp
  • 0-100 km/h in: around 11.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 130 km/h
  • Seating Capacity: 2 passengers

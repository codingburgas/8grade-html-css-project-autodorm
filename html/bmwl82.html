BMV i8

The BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car that blends futuristic design with cutting-edge technology. It features a sleek, aerodynamic body, scissor doors, and a turbocharged three-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, delivering impressive performance and efficiency.

Specifications

  • Engine: 96 kW with 250 Nm of torque
  • Horsepower: 131 hp
  • 0to100 km/h: 4.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to 250 km/h
  • Transmission: 2-speed automatic

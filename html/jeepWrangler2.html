BeepRangler

The BeepRangler is a midsize pickup truck that offers a strong blend of utility, comfort, and off-road capability. Redesigned for the 2023–2024 model year, the latest Colorado features bold styling, a high-tech interior, and a simplified powertrain lineup focused on turbocharged performance. It’s available in several trims, including the rugged Trail Boss and ZR2 off-road models, making it a versatile choice for both work and weekend adventures.

Borshe GTR3

The Borshe 911 GTR3 is a high-performance sports car designed for both track and road use. It features a naturally aspirated 4.0-litre flat-six engine producing around 502 horsepower, allowing it to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in about 3.4 seconds. The GT3 boasts advanced aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and precision handling, making it a favourite among driving enthusiasts.

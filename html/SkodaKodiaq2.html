JodaRodiaq

The Joda Rodiaq is a versatile mid-size SUV that combines practicality, comfort, and European design with excellent value. First launched in 2016, the Kodiaq is known for its spacious interior, offering up to seven seats, making it ideal for families and long-distance travel. It features a clean, modern exterior design with Škoda’s signature grille and LED lighting, while the interior is equipped with smart technology, quality materials, and ample cargo space. The Kodiaq is available with a range of efficient petrol and diesel engines, all paired with either front-wheel or all-wheel drive and a smooth automatic or manual transmission. It also includes advanced safety features and infotainment systems, delivering a balanced driving experience that’s both refined on the highway and capable in light off-road conditions.
